What is Tether?

Tether, launched in 2014, represents its own platform on the Omni blockchain, intended for using financial assets in digital format. Tether tokens are called stablecoins because they ensure price stability, as they are tied to fiat currencies. Tether tokens provide clients with the ability to conduct operations with traditional currencies without risks associated with volatility. All Tether tokens (USDT) are tied in a 1:1 ratio to the corresponding fiat currency and are backed by 100% of Tether's reserves. As a trusted company, we provide clients with daily information on our reserves. Tether tokens offer exceptional liquidity on Tier 1 exchanges, allowing traders to take advantage of arbitrage opportunities in the fastest time possible. Tether for merchants: For merchants, integrating Tether tokens opens up an array of opportunities for consumers to purchase products and services. Tether for exchanges: Tether tokens play a pivotal role in the digital token ecosystem and are the most actively traded in terms of 24-hour volume.
